I build web products that balance **product clarity**, **engineering quality**, and long-term maintainability. My work usually sits at the intersection of frontend architecture, design systems, and backend integration, with a strong bias toward shipping practical systems that teams can iterate on quickly.

Over the last few years, I have focused on building internal platforms, developer-facing tools, and data-heavy interfaces. I enjoy turning ambiguous requirements into interfaces that feel calm, intentional, and easy to trust.

## What I care about

- shipping clean interfaces that are easy to scan and maintain
- building components and content systems that scale with minimal overhead
- keeping the path from idea → prototype → production short
- making technical products easier for teams and users to understand

## Working style

I prefer a workflow where content, structure, and presentation are clearly separated. That is why I like markdown-driven setups for personal sites and documentation: they make updates fast, versionable, and low-friction.

```ts
const mindset = {
  clarity: 'make the structure obvious',
  maintainability: 'prefer simple systems over clever systems',
  delivery: 'ship something useful, then refine it'
};
```
